Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order, provided the order has not yet been shipped.

Once an order has been shipped, cancellation requests will not be accepted.

To cancel an order, customers must contact customer support with their order details.

If an order is cancelled successfully, any applicable refund will be processed according to the Refund Policy.

The company reserves the right to cancel orders in cases of payment failure, stock unavailability, or suspected fraudulent activity.
