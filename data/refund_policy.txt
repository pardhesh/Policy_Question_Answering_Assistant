Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a refund within 14 days from the date of delivery.

Refunds are applicable only for products that are unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.

Shipping charges and handling fees are non-refundable.

Once the returned item is received and inspected, the refund will be processed within 7 business days.

Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment used during purchase.

Items purchased during promotional sales or special discounts are not eligible for refunds.
