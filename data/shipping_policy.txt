Shipping Policy

Orders are typically processed within 2 to 3 business days after confirmation.

Standard domestic shipping takes approximately 5 to 7 business days.

Shipping timelines may vary depending on location, courier service, or unforeseen circumstances.

Currently, shipping services are available only within the country.

Tracking information will be shared with customers once the order has been dispatched.

The company is not responsible for delays caused by natural disasters, strikes, or courier service disruptions.
